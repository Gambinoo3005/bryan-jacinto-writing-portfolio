Uragon Transport Services is a car rental company in the Bicol Region of the Philippines. I built their website end to end: the frontend design, the backend, the SEO setup, and the copy that ties it all together.

## What I handled

- **Design and frontend.** A clean, mobile-friendly site built around what a rental customer actually needs: vehicles, rates, and a fast way to get in touch.
- **Backend and deployment.** Built out and shipped on Vercel.
- **SEO.** On-page optimization so the business surfaces for local rental searches.
- **Copywriting.** Clear, conversion-focused copy across the pages, written to read like a real local business rather than a template.

This one is a good example of the full range I can cover on a single project: design, build, SEO, and copy, all from one person.
